Asia

Asia, my home continent, also happens to be the largest ones in terms of land mass AND population. It saw the beginning of many of humanity's first civilizations. A continent known for its culture, history, and traditions. Here are some of the destinations closest to my country that I plan to go to.



National Center for Performing Arts

Now this is the kind of place that when I finally set foot in, I'd be able to tell myself "I made it!". The National Center for Performing Arts in Beijing, China, also known as "The Giant Egg" (lame nickname if you ask me lmao), is an architectural marvel featuring a unique ellipsoidal dome of titanium and glass. It covers an area of about 219,400 square meters and houses 3 performance venues: The Opera House, The Concert Hall, and the Theater.

Furano Flower Fields

I am a fan of all things Japan, and with my newfound appreciation of flowers, this place sounds perfect place for me. The Furano Flower Fields, located in Hokkaido, Japan, are known for its organized and stunning displays of various flowers with different colors, most notable of them is the Lavender.

Ta Pa Rice Fields

Fort Santiago

If you're a Filipino, you know this. From our very own country, Fort Santiago of Intramuros, Manila in the Philippines stands as one of the most historically significant sites in the country. After all, this is the very place in which the most popular and most known hero of the Philippines, was imprisoned. Now it serves as a reminder of our history, of the sacrifices that our ancestor went through so that us, their descendants, can have a taste of freedom. The fort consists of a Main Gate, Moat and Bastions, as well as a shrine dedicated to Rizal himself.
